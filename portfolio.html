Till Death: College Film Poster

For a college assignment the interactive media student were given the task of doing project for students in the film programe. The film students are clients to the media students and they would ask the media students to do something that would contribute to their film. I was assigned to do a poster. The client asked for colors of white white black and red. The film was more of a tragedy so I gave it this grungy feel to set the mood of the film. The brushes used in this piece are blood splatter and ink drops. I gave it a grey gradient to keep the background from looking plain and it helps blend the red in with the piece

Video Effects: Trapcode Particular

The follwing is a picture of video footage, it is simulation of particles being distrted through wind physics. The camera is focused at the core of the emitter and as the particles get closer to the camera they start to fade. It is essentially for an art show to be projected on a side of a cube along with other looping videos showcasing different patterns and video footage.
